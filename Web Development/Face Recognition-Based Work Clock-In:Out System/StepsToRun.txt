Step 1: Run the Application
python main.py


Step 2: Register a New User
Click the New User button.
Enter a username in the provided text box.
The application captures your face and saves it for future recognition.


Step 3: Login
Click the Login button.
Look at the webcam. The application detects your face.
If recognized, you'll see a welcome message, and your attendance will be logged.
If unrecognized, you'll be prompted to register.
